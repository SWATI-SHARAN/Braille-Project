How It Works

SpeakDots uses advanced Deep Learning and Computer Vision to bridge the communication gap.

1

Image Preprocessing

When you upload an image, our system first cleans it. We apply noise reduction, thresholding, and contour detection to isolate the individual Braille dots from the paper texture.

2

Character Segmentation

The system identifies each Braille cell (a 2x3 grid of dots). It slices the image into individual character blocks, preparing them for the recognition engine.

3

CNN Recognition

A Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on thousands of Braille samples analyzes each cell. It predicts the corresponding English letter with high accuracy.

4

Text Reconstruction

The designated characters are stitched back together into words and sentences. Finally, our Text-to-Speech engine converts this text into spoken audio.
